#Battle#

#0
|By battling your opponents, you can improve on multiple different aspects.;

|Every battle is against an opponent in the same division as you are in. (see leaderboard for divisions);

|Every game mode, has it's own corresponding skills and amount of elo and fame that will be altered.;

|The amount of points you have in the corresponding skills, determines your chance to win.;

|Skill influence for each game mode:
1v1: Game Knowledge & Mechanics

3v3: Game Knowledge & Team Play

5v5: Game Knowledge & Mechanics & Team Play;

#5
|After every battle you will increase your skills, even if you lose.;

|By winning battles from your opponents, you will increase your ranking and popularity.;

|But lose battles, and you will decrease your ranking and popularity.;
